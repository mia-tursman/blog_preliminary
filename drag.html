Drag Show

☘︎

Weekly Drag show at The George club

Overview

  • This event happens on Wednesdays at “The George” in Dublin
  • It lasted about 2 hours and started at around 9:30 or 10 pm
  • It was super fun and engaging

My Experience

On Wednesday night, we went to The George–a gay club in Dublin– for their “Witchy Wednesday” weekly event, which included a drag show. It was super fun, and the performers were very impressive. I would definitely recommend!
