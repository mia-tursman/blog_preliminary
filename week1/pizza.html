Detroit style pizza in Dublin

Overview

  • Food Style: Pizza and Bar
  • Suitable for: Large Groups
  • Takeout: Yes
  • Address: 14 Dame Ln, Dublin 2, D02 XC91
View Google Overview

My Experience

This pub is located in Greystones, just south of Dublin by train. It had a very cute outdoor seating area where we were able to order drinks and enjoy their patio. I had a delicious Aperol Spritz as well as a cheeseburger with fries. The place was very empty when we went, making the inside feel dull, however the outdoor seating was very peaceful.
