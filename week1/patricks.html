St. Patricks Park

☘︎

Sitting outside St. Patricks Cathedral

Overview

  • Duration: As long as you please
  • A large park surrounding St. Patricks cathedral
  • Benches and green space to relax in while overlooking the cathedral

My Experience

Every once in a while, on the walk back to my apartment from the classroom building, I will stop in this park to sit and stare at the beautiful stone cathedral. There are many benches, as well as a cafe, and it is an amazing area to people-watch. It is fascinating to me that I am living in an area where I can walk 10 minutes and stare at such a beautiful building.
