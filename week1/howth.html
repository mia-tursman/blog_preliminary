Howth Cliff Walk

☘︎

Completing the Howth Cliff Walk

Overview

  • Duration: 5 hours
  • Travel Logistics: 30-40 minute DART train north from Dublin
  • Take the train from Tara St. Station toward Howth until the end of the line
  • This trip is on the LEAP System

My Experience

I took the train from Dublin to Howth to complete the Howth cliff walk in Howth, Ireland. Upon arriving, I bought the sheep keychains featured in the photo gallery on the home page. After purchasing these, I walked the cliff walk, which took about three hours. I listened to EDM music and walked along the water, and it was one of my favorite days here. The hike ends at a beautiful lighthouse overlook, and I walked back through the town afterward.
