Brother Hubbard

☘︎

Sit down brunch in Dublin

Overview

  • Food Style: Sit down brunch
  • Suitable for: Medium-size groups
  • Takeout: Yes, but preferable to sit-in
  • Address: 153 Capel St, North City, Dublin, D01 V9V0
View Google Overview

My Experience

We visited this restaurant in a group of seven and were seated immediately. This place had a lovely aesthetic, and the group got a variety of meals, including: pastries, coffees, soups, and wraps! I personally got the curry chicken wrap with the potato soup on the side. It was a perfect and easy brunch place for our large group.
