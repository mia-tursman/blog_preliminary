The Beanhive

☘︎

Cute Cafe in Dublin

Overview

  • Food Style: Cafe
  • Suitable for: Small Groups
  • Takeout: Yes
  • Address: 26 Dawson St, Dublin 2, D02 FY28
View Google Overview

My Experience

This cafe is one of my favorites in all of Dublin. It has two locations, and both have great outdoor and indoor seating for smaller groups. This place can get busy due to the extremely cute latte art designs, however it is worth the wait for the aesthetic of the drinks and the reasonable prices. It does not cost extra to add the latte art to a hot drink, which can be taken to go or in person, and a latte runs at about 5 euro.
