One Kinda Folk

☘︎

Grab and go sandwiches in Dublin

Overview

  • Food Style: Coffee and Takeaway
  • Suitable for: Small groups
  • Takeout: Yes
  • Address: 126 Leeson Street Upper, Dublin 4, D04 WY62
View Google Overview

My Experience

On our first day in Dublin, we visited the restaurant One Kinda Folk. Although it was tight inside and we had a large group, we were able to grab delicious sandwiches and coffee and take them to a nearby park to eat. I had a focaccia sandwich, which was premade, and it was delicious and extremely easy.
