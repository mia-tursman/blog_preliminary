Dudley's

☘︎

Authentic pub with food in The Liberties, Dublin

Overview

  • Food Style: Pub
  • Suitable for: All size groups!
  • Takeout: No
  • Address: 48 Thomas St, The Liberties, Dublin, D08 Y44A
View Google Overview

My Experience

We have been to this pub multiple times as it is extremely close to our apartment. It is great for burgers, guinness pints, and ciders (especially Rockshore and Orchard Thieves). It has a dimly lit, authentic Irish feel with great, mellow live music.
