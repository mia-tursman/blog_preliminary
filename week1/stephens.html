St. Stephens Green

☘︎

Reading in St. Stephens Green

Overview

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • A large park in the center of Dublin
  • Many people nap, read, walk, and chat

My Experience

My birthday was spent here in Dublin, and it was one of the first sunny days of my three weeks here. I went to St. Stephen's Green after getting coffee at Beanhive Cafe. I laid in the park for hours reading my book and observing all the ways that the local people spent their free time. There were groups of people having picnics, drinking wine, staring at the sky, and watching their kids run around in the grass. I would highly recommend a visit to this park on a sunny day in Dublin.
