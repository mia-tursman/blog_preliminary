Sushi in Dublin

Overview

  • Food Style: Sushi dinner
  • Suitable for: Small Groups
  • Takeout: Yes
  • Address: Apartment 5, Bond St, Saint Catherine's, Dublin, D08 YV8A
View Website

My Experience

We went to this place hoping to sit and eat dinner, however they were on a two hour wait. If you are looking to visit here, I would suggest getting a reservation, or we got takeout. I got a salmon, cream cheese roll as well as pork gyoza and both were delicious and the perfect takeout option in the liberties. This meal was affordable as sushi goes, and it ended up being about 20 euros.
