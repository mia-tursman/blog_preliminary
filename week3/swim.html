The Forty Foot

☘︎

Swimming spot in Dublin

Overview

  • Duration: 1 hour, could stay for longer
  • Travel: 30 minute train ride from Dublin
  • Very busy on sunny days, but still fun!

My Experience

The final few days in Dublin ended up extremly sunny, so we hopped on the train and went to the Forty Foot. This is a spot with a rocky, and cliff heavy shoreline with options to wade in or jump off of the rock. The water was very cold and it was very crowded, but this was still a super fun spot to cool off on a hot afternoon! We stayed for about an hour because we had a whiskey cocktail making class afterward, but it was a lovely day!
