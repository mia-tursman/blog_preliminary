Bray Cliff Walk

☘︎

Walk along the coast from Bray to Greystones

Overview

  • Duration: 2-hour hike
  • Travel Logistics: 40-minute train south to Bray on the LEAP card system
  • Start the hike in Bray, end in Greystones, and take the train back from Greystones

My Experience

I started my day by taking the train from Dublin to Bray, where I got coffee at Finbees Coffee House coffee house. From there, I started the hike in Bray. This hike is gorgeous and follows the coast and the train tracks all the way to Greystones. There is a section that has been closed off for the past couple of years. However, the trail was safe, and locals told me to go around the closed-off fence to complete the hike. The trail was not too difficult, and ended in Greystones, where you then take the train back to Dublin.
