Finbees Coffee House

☘︎

Cute Cafe in Bray

Overview

  • Food Style: Cafe
  • Suitable for: Large Groups
  • Takeout: Yes
  • Address: The Seafront, Victoria Ave, Bray, Co. Wicklow, A98 N8N3
View Google Overview

My Experience

This cafe was very cute located in the small town of Bray, which is south of Dublin. I got an iced vanilla latte and an eclair and both were not the best I have had, but good. The cafe is cute and has outdoor and indoor seating overlooking the water. I recommend sitting upstairs, as this has the best panoramic water views.
