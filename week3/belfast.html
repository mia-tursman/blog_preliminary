Northern Ireland

☘︎

Bus tour through Northern Ireland

Overview

  • Duration: 12 hours
  • I booked a day-long bus tour through “Get Your Guide”
  • We stopped at: The Dark Hedges, the Giant's Causeway, and Belfast

My Experience

This tour was awesome and cost us about $80. We boarded the bus at 6:30 AM and drove to the dark hedges first, which is a location in the Game of Thrones. While these were a bit underwhelming, it was still cool to see. We then went to the Giant's Causeway, which was the absolute highlight of my entire trip. We had lovely weather, and the views of the incredible rock formations and cliffs were amazing; you could even see the Scottish coast in the distance! Next, we drove to Belfast, where we were only able to spend about an hour and a half, which was a little disappointing, but still cool to explore. This tour was an amazing way to quickly see the main highlights of Northern Ireland, and I would definitely recommend it!
