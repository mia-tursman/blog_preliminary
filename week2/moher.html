Cliffs of Moher

☘︎

Bus ride day trip to the Cliffs of Moher

Overview

  • Travel Logistics: 3hours from Dublin, about 1.5 hours from Galway
  • Took the bus from Dublin to Galway, then one from Galway to the cliffs
  • Beautiful green cliffs with steep drop-offs

My Experience

After a weekend in Galway, we took a bus to the Cliffs of Moher, on the west coast of Ireland. The bus ride was very windy and made me a little car sick, but it was worth it. After arriving, I walked along the cliffs and looked out over the Atlantic Ocean. It was very rainy and windy, but the views were beautiful, and it was one of the most memorable places I visited in Ireland.
