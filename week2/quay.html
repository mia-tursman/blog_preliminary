The Quay Pub

☘︎

Fun pub in Galway

Overview

  • Food Style: Pub
  • Suitable for: Any size group
  • Takeout: No
  • Address: Quay Ln, Galway
View Website

My Experience

This was a very fun and lively pub in Galway. We went with a large group, and, while the place was very crowded, we were able to meet a lot of new people. There was very good live music, and we got pints of guinness. This is the main tourist bar in Galway and is the heart of night-life in the city.
