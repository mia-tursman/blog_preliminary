☘︎

Pasta restaurant in Galway

Overview

  • Food Style: Pasta
  • Suitable for: Small Groups
  • Takeout: Yes
  • Address: 13 Mary St, Galway, H91 K4X2
View Website

My Experience

This pasta restaurant located in Galway was our favorite food of the trip. It was very small inside, so I would recommend going in groups of 2-4 only.I got the bolognese with rigatoni, and it was delicious. The portion sizes were large, and the place was very cute with great vegan options.
